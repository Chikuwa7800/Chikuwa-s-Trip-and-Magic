## Changes
<p>Reduced the flight time of Bolt Strike and Zephyr Blade.</p>
<p>Toned down Level 1 magic effects for better visibility.</p>
<p>Updated the Zephyr Blade's terrain hit effects and sounds.</p>
<p>Reduced the volume of Bolt Strike's hit sound.</p>
<p>Some terrain hit effects and sounds of all level 1 magics will now only play when hitting an entity.</p>
<p>Mana capacity and regeneration now scale with the player's highest reached XP level.</p>
<p>Reduced the max mana bonus from Magic Core [Add Mana capacity] (50 → 10).</p>
<p>The Japanese spelling of "Mana" was changed from "Mana" to "マナ".</p>

<p>Fixed an issue where mobs failed to be lanched when killed by the Atter Raggio.</p>
